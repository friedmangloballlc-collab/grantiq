## Pricing Sweetspots by Organization Size

### Nonprofits Under $500K Annual Budget
**Recommended:** Free tier → OpenGrants ($29/mo) or Grantboost ($20/mo)
**Rationale:** Low budget; discovery and AI writing most valuable; minimal team collaboration needed

### Nonprofits $500K–$5M Annual Budget
**Recommended:** Granted AI ($57/mo) or Grantable Pro ($68–$99/mo)
**Rationale:** Can afford mid-range; need both discovery and AI writing; may have 2–3 person grant team

### Nonprofits $5M–$50M Annual Budget
**Recommended:** Candid/FDO Professional ($170–$220/mo) + Instrumentl ($299/mo)
**Rationale:** Need enterprise data + professional discovery + team features

### Nonprofits $50M+ or Foundations
**Recommended:** Submittable or Fluxx ($custom) or Grant Assistant ($custom)
**Rationale:** Need complete grant operations platform; dedicated support; custom integrations
