## Key Findings Summary

### Top Insights

1. **AI Grant Writing is Now Standard** (57% of market)
   - 8 of 14 platforms include AI writing as of 2026
   - Market shifted significantly from 2024 (only 2-3 platforms)

2. **Freemium Models Dominate New Entrants**
   - 9 of 14 platforms (64%) offer free tiers
   - Conversion rate: 3-5% from free to paid

3. **Price Clustering is Clear**
   - Under $50/mo: Freemium/SMB (6 platforms)
   - $50-$100/mo: Professional (4 platforms)
   - $100-$300/mo: Mid-market (2 platforms)
   - Enterprise/Custom: (4 platforms)

4. **No Platform Bundles Well**
   - Most platforms specialize (discovery OR writing, not both)
   - Only OpenGrants and Instrumentl attempt bundling
   - **Gap:** Single platform with integrated discovery + writing

5. **Success-Based Pricing Gap** (Huge opportunity)
   - ZERO competitors charge based on grants won
   - All use subscription-only model
   - Risk reversal available (Granted AI's "win guarantee")

### Market Opportunities for GrantIQ

1. **Bundle Discovery + AI Writing** at $79-$199/mo
   - Instrumentl does discovery only ($299/mo)
   - Grantboost does writing only ($20/mo)
   - Combine both for competitive advantage

2. **Freemium-to-Premium Funnel**
   - Offer generous free tier (10 AI writes/month)
   - Convert to Professional at $79/mo
   - Upsell Team tier at $199/mo

3. **Success-Based Option** (Year 2)
   - $79/mo base + 5-10% of grants won (min $10K grant)
   - Only differentiator from competitors
   - Unique risk-sharing model

4. **Network/Volume Discounts**
   - Only GrantStation offers partnerships
   - Gap: Grant networks, nonprofit coalitions, foundation cohorts
   - Opportunity: 25-35% volume discounts at 10+ organizations

5. **Vertical Specialization**
   - All competitors are horizontal (any grant type)
   - Opportunity: Education-focused, healthcare-focused, STEM-focused grants at premium pricing
