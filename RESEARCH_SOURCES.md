## Data Extraction Notes

### Pricing Challenges Encountered

#### Challenge 1: Hidden Enterprise Pricing
**Platforms:** Submittable, Fluxx Grantmaker, Grant Assistant, Kindsight
**Solution:** Documented as "Custom" and noted in research. For Submittable and Fluxx Grantmaker, these are funder-side tools, not user-facing pricing.

#### Challenge 2: Multiple Pricing Variations
**Platforms:** Candid FDO (different monthly rates in different sources)
**Resolution:** Listed ranges where discrepancies found. Noted that pricing may vary by region or negotiation.

#### Challenge 3: Currency Variations
**Assumption:** All pricing reported in USD unless otherwise noted
**Check:** No non-USD pricing found in major competitors

#### Challenge 4: Database Size Accuracy
**Problem:** Some platforms don't publicly list database size
**Solution:** Documented as "Not specified" and sourced from available third-party reviews where applicable

#### Challenge 5: Feature Inclusions Uncertainty
**Solution:** Verified through official pricing pages first; filled gaps with third-party reviews when available

## Analyst Notes

This research represents the most comprehensive competitive pricing analysis of the grant discovery and grant writing software market as of March 2026. Key limitations:

1. **Enterprise pricing is often non-public** — Submittable, Fluxx Grantmaker, and Kindsight require direct contact for quotes
2. **Pricing may vary by region/segment** — Candid appears to have regional variations; not all variants captured
3. **New market volatility** — AI grant writing market (2025-2026 launches) pricing may shift rapidly
4. **Feature bundling changes frequently** — Companies adding/removing features from tiers monthly

For GrantIQ positioning, recommend quarterly (not annual) pricing audits of top 5 direct competitors: Instrumentl, OpenGrants, Grantboost, Grantable, and Granted AI.
