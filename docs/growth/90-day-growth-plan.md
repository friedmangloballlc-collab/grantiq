## 1. TOP 3 ACQUISITION CHANNELS (First 100 Users)

### Channel 1: LinkedIn Outbound to Nonprofit Leaders (Expected: 40-50 users)

**Why:** Nonprofits with $250K-$5M budgets are the sweet spot. They need grants desperately but cannot afford Instrumentl ($149/mo) or a full-time grant writer ($65K+/yr). These organizations have Executive Directors and Development Directors who are active on LinkedIn.

**Exact targeting:**
- Title filters: "Executive Director" OR "Development Director" OR "Grants Manager" OR "Director of Development" OR "Chief Development Officer"
- Organization size: 5-50 employees
- Industries: Education, Healthcare, Social Services, Arts/Culture, Environmental, Community Development
- Geography: Start with top 10 metro areas (NYC, LA, Chicago, Houston, Phoenix, Philadelphia, San Antonio, San Diego, Dallas, Austin)

**Execution cadence (from Friedman Global Playbook):**
- Week 1-2: Build target list of 500 prospects using LinkedIn Sales Navigator
- Send 50 connection requests/week with personalized notes referencing their org's mission
- After connection: Send value-first DM linking to their industry's grant directory page (e.g., "I noticed [Org] works in youth education -- we just published a free database of 47 education grants with open deadlines")
- Follow up non-responders at Day 3 and Day 7
- Goal: 10 discovery calls/week, converting 30% to trial, 50% of trials to paid

**Message template:**
```
Hi [Name] -- I saw [Org]'s work in [specific program]. Impressive impact.

We built a free tool that shows funding gaps for orgs like yours:
[link to funding-gap calculator pre-filled with their industry]

No pitch, just thought it might be useful for your next board meeting.
```

**Metrics:**
- Connection acceptance rate: 25-35%
- DM response rate: 10-15%
- Discovery call booking rate: 30% of responses
- Trial conversion: 50%
- Paid conversion: 40% of trials
- Math: 500 connections x 30% accept x 12% respond x 30% call x 50% trial x 40% paid = ~27 users/month

| 5         | 3 months free + "Champion"   | 1 month free           |
| 10        | 6 months free + AI Writing   | 1 month free           |
| 25        | Free for life (current tier) | 1 month free           |

**Viral loops to build:**

**Loop 1: The Funding Gap Report Share Loop**
1. User runs Funding Gap Calculator
2. Gets a shareable "Funding Gap Report" with their org's score
3. Report has "See how your org compares to others in [Industry]" link
4. Link goes to a landing page showing anonymized industry benchmarks
5. CTA: "Run your own Funding Gap Analysis"
6. New user enters the loop

**Expected K-factor: 0.3-0.5** (each user brings 0.3-0.5 new users)

**Loop 2: The Grant Match Share Loop**
1. User gets matched with grants
2. "Share this grant with a colleague" button on each grant match

1. Weekly digest email includes "Know someone who needs this grant?"

**Expected K-factor: 0.1-0.2**

**Loop 4: The Leaderboard Competition Loop**
1. Monthly "Top Referrer" wins a free AI-Written Grant Narrative ($1,749 value)

## 6. PRICING PAGE OPTIMIZATION

### Current: 4 tiers. Recommendation: Restructure for higher conversion.

**Problems to solve:**
- Too many choices creates decision paralysis (Hick's Law)
- The "Full Confidence Package" ($0 upfront, 10% success fee) should be PROMINENTLY featured -- it is the most disruptive offer in the market
- AI Writing pricing needs to feel like an add-on, not a barrier

**Recommended pricing page structure:**

```
HEADER: "Find and Win Grants. Pay Only When You Succeed."
SUBHEADER: "1,203+ grants. AI matching. AI writing. $0 risk."

[Toggle: Monthly / Annual (save 20%)]

TIER 1: DISCOVER          TIER 2: PROFESSIONAL        TIER 3: FULL CONFIDENCE
$49/mo                     $149/mo (MOST POPULAR)      $0/mo + 10% success fee

- 25 grant matches/mo      - Unlimited matches          - Everything in Pro
- Basic readiness score     - Advanced readiness + AI    - AI-written proposals
- Weekly digest            - Strategy recommendations    - Expert review
- Grant directory access    - Priority new grants        - Dedicated strategist
                           - Funding gap analysis        - $0 upfront cost
                                                        - Pay only when you win

[Start Free Trial]         [Start Free Trial]           [Apply Now]
```

**Key changes:**
1. Reduce to 3 tiers (not 4) -- the paradox of choice kills conversion
2. Make "Full Confidence" the anchor on the right -- even if few qualify, it reframes the other tiers as affordable
3. Add social proof under each tier: "[X] organizations chose this plan"
4. Add "Recommended for you" badge on the Professional tier
5. Feature comparison table below the cards (collapsed by default on mobile)
6. Add FAQ section addressing objections:
   - "What if I don't win any grants?"
   - "How is this different from Instrumentl?"
   - "Can I switch plans?"
   - "What counts as a 'success fee'?"

**Conversion boosters:**
- Add annual pricing toggle with "Save 20%" badge
- Show "per grant found" pricing equivalent: "$149/mo = $6/grant" (if avg user gets 25 matches)
- Add "Risk-Free" badge on all tiers (14-day free trial)
- Testimonial carousel between pricing cards and FAQ
- Exit-intent popup: "Not sure which plan? Take our 60-second quiz"

**A/B tests to run:**
1. 3 tiers vs 4 tiers
2. Monthly default vs Annual default
3. "Start Free Trial" vs "Get Matched Now" CTA text
4. With/without the "Full Confidence" tier visible
5. Price anchoring: showing "Equivalent to hiring a grant writer at $65K/yr" comparison
