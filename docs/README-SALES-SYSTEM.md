
## Critical Success Factors

### 1. Heavy Personalization
Every cold email must be hand-researched (5 min per prospect). Spend time learning about their org, recent grants, sector trends. This is what drives 3-5x higher reply rates.

**Where:** sales-automation-system.md (Section 1), sales-operational-playbook.md (Cold email launch checklist)

### 2. Strict Qualification
Use A-Z framework to disqualify bad-fit prospects early. Only pursue leads that meet all 6 criteria. This keeps conversion rates high (40-50% on consultations).

**Where:** sales-automation-system.md (Section 5), sales-operational-playbook.md (Discovery call script)

### 3. Multi-Touch Persistence
First email gets opens, not replies. Third email gets replies. Fourth email (phone call) books meetings. Don't give up after one email.

**Where:** sales-automation-system.md (Section 2), sales-operational-playbook.md (Follow-up cadence)

### 4. Automation + Human Touch
Automate the repetitive parts (email sequences, scheduling, lead scoring). Keep the human parts (research, discovery calls, proposals, closing).

**Where:** sales-automation-system.md (Section 6), crm-automation-configuration.md

### 5. Daily KPI Tracking
Measure everything: emails sent, opens, clicks, replies, discovery calls, conversions. Track weekly, adjust based on data.

**Where:** sales-launch-implementation-plan.md (Measurement & Reporting)

### 6. Sales Manager Coaching
Sales manager should listen to discovery calls, review proposals, coach on objection handling. First-time sales people need feedback.

**Where:** sales-operational-playbook.md, sales-launch-implementation-plan.md (Week 6 training)

## Common Questions

### Q: How long until this is profitable?
**A:** Break-even by Month 2 (Week 6-8). First deal closes by Week 4. By Week 12, you'll have closed $75K-$125K in deals to cover the $60K+ investment.

**See:** SALES-SYSTEM-EXECUTIVE-SUMMARY.md (Revenue Model)

### Q: Do we need to hire new sales people?
**A:** Ideally yes (1-2 dedicated sales reps). But you can start with an existing team member if needed. A single person can handle 50 cold emails/week + 10 discovery calls/week if they're organized.

**See:** sales-launch-implementation-plan.md (Week 1-2 hiring)

### Q: What CRM should we use?
**A:** HubSpot Sales Hub ($180/user/month) or Pipedrive ($39-165/user/month). Both work well. HubSpot has better automation built-in.

**See:** crm-automation-configuration.md (Part 1)

### Q: What if our email reply rate is low?
**A:** Test 3 new subject lines. Improve personalization. Swap to higher-quality prospect list. Check deliverability (not going to spam). See "Contingency Plans" for full diagnostics.

**See:** sales-launch-implementation-plan.md (Contingency: Low email open rates)

### Q: How do we handle objections?
**A:** We have 5 common objections with scripts for each. Follow the script, reframe the objection as a question, offer alternative solution.

**See:** sales-operational-playbook.md (Objection Handling Scripts)

### Q: Can we use this for other products?
**A:** Yes, this system is generic enough for any B2B consulting service. Just replace the sequences with your specific pain points and value props.

**See:** sales-automation-system.md (Sections 1-2, customize as needed)
