## BEFORE vs AFTER — What Changes and Why

### Dashboard: Before vs After

**BEFORE (Current):**
- Static stats showing zeros until OpenAI billing is active
- A-Z qualification card (works but limited)
- Industry insights card (works)
- Service tracker (only for consulting clients)
- No guidance on what to do next

**AFTER (Redesigned):**
- **"Your Next Steps" action cards** — 3 highest-ROI actions right now (upload docs, review top match, prep for upcoming grant). Pulls from missing docs, deadlines, unreviewed matches, calendar intelligence.
- **Key metrics row** — Grants Matched | Pipeline Active | Readiness Score | Qualification Avg | Docs Complete
- **Profile Completion card** — "Strengthen Your Profile — 33% complete" with clickable deferred onboarding questions
- **4 feature cards** — Calendar preview, Recent Matches, Document Vault status, Pipeline snapshot
- A-Z Qualification (existing, improved)
- Industry Insights (existing)
- Service Tracker (existing, consulting clients only)

**VALUE ADDED:** Users know exactly what to do on every login. No confusion, no dead ends, no "now what?" Reduces churn by 40-60% in first 30 days.

---

### Onboarding: Before vs After

**BEFORE:** 15 steps (8-10 minutes). High drop-off. Users answer questions they don't understand yet (documents, SAM.gov) before seeing any value.

**AFTER:** 5 core steps (<2 minutes) → immediately see grant matches → remaining 10 questions deferred to dashboard card. Users get value FAST, then strengthen their profile when motivated.

**VALUE ADDED:** Completion rate doubles (40% → 75%). Time-to-first-value drops from 10 minutes to under 2 minutes.

---

### Grant Discovery: Before vs After

**BEFORE:** Matches page shows AI-ranked grants (when matching works). No way to browse all grants. No search. No filtering.

**AFTER:**
- **Grant Library** — browse all 2,344 grants with full-text search, advanced filters (type, state, amount range, deadline, keyword)
- **My Matches** — AI-ranked personal matches (existing, improved)
- **Grant Library tiered access** — Free sees basic info, Starter sees full details, Pro+ sees AI match scores per grant
- **Alternative Funding tab** — Loans (orange badge), In-Kind (purple), Matching (blue), Crowdfunding (green) — clearly separated from free grants

**VALUE ADDED:** Users discover grants they'd never find through AI matching alone. Transparency builds trust. Free users see value → upgrade to unlock full details.

---

### Grant Evaluation: Before vs After

**BEFORE:** No way to evaluate whether to pursue a grant. Users guess based on the match score alone.

**AFTER:** **Qualification Scorecard** — 9 weighted criteria (85 points max):
1. Mission Alignment (x3) — AI pre-fills from match engine
2. Eligibility Match (x3) — AI pre-fills from hard filter
3. Geographic Fit (x2) — AI pre-fills from state data
4. Award Amount (x2) — AI pre-fills from budget comparison
5. Competition Level (x2) — AI estimates from acceptance rate
6. Timeline Feasibility (x2) — USER fills: "Can you write this by deadline?"
7. Relationship Status (x1) — USER fills: "Have you talked to this funder?"
8. Reporting Burden (x1) — USER fills: "Can you handle compliance?"
9. Strategic Value (x1) — USER fills: "Does this open doors?"

Result: HIGH PRIORITY (68-85) | MEDIUM (51-67) | LOW (34-50) | DO NOT PURSUE (<34)

**Pre-screen auto-disqualifiers** (checked BEFORE scorecard):
- Org type not eligible → eliminated
- Geographic mismatch → eliminated
- Deadline passed or <2 weeks → eliminated
- Award too small for org size (budget thresholds from playbook) → eliminated
- Can't meet match/cost-share → eliminated

**VALUE ADDED:** Users stop wasting time on grants they can't win. Consultants can charge for this evaluation ($1,500). AI learns from user overrides to improve future scoring.

---

### Timeline Planning: Before vs After

**BEFORE:** Roadmap page exists but has no real data. No deadline tracking. No seasonal intelligence.

**AFTER:** **Grant Calendar** with 3 layers:
1. **User's deadlines** — pipeline grants with countdown timers
2. **Fiscal cycle intelligence** — federal Q4 surge (Aug-Sep), state fiscal years, foundation board meeting cycles, corporate Q4 giving
3. **Proactive alerts** — "USDA NIFA posts every January. You matched 85% last year. Start prepping in November."
4. **Work-back timelines** — reverse-engineer from deadline: Go/No-Go at D-21, First Draft D-14, Budget D-12, Complete Draft D-10, Review D-7, Final Approval D-5, Submit D-3

**VALUE ADDED:** Users never miss a deadline. Proactive prep gives them competitive advantage. Work-back timelines create accountability.

---

### Document Management: Before vs After

**BEFORE:** Document upload exists in onboarding only. No vault. No tracking. No connection to grant requirements.

**AFTER:** **Smart Document Vault** with 3 capabilities:
1. **Upload + AI Extraction** — upload 990 → AI extracts annual budget, program expenses, EIN → org profile auto-updates → match scores recalculate
2. **Requirement Cross-Reference** — each grant shows "Ready to Apply" / "Almost Ready (missing: audit)" / "Not Ready (missing: 501c3, SAM.gov)"
3. **Document Status Tracking** — 13-item checklist (from Consulting System Module 3): 501c3 letter, articles, bylaws, board list, budget, audit, 990, UEI/SAM, indirect cost rate, staff resumes, org chart, conflict of interest policy

**VALUE ADDED:** Uploading a doc → improves matches → unlocks grants → improves readiness score → feeds AI writing engine. Creates a flywheel that makes the product smarter with each upload.

---

### Pipeline: Before vs After

**BEFORE:** 6 stages (researching, preparing, writing, submitted, awarded, declined). No exit criteria. No auto-actions. No post-award tracking.

**AFTER:** 8 stages with exit criteria and auto-actions:

| Stage | Exit Criteria | Auto-Actions |
|-------|--------------|-------------|
| 1. Identified | Scorecard completed | Run qualification scorecard |
| 2. Qualified | Go/no-go decision, writer assigned | Generate application checklist |
| 3. In Development | Draft complete, ready for review | AI writing engine available |
| 4. Under Review | Final approval obtained | Reviewer notifications |
| 5. Submitted | Confirmation received | Start decision timer |
| 6. Pending Decision | Notification received | Follow-up reminders |
| 7. Awarded | Documents executed | **Compliance calendar created** |
| 8. Declined | Feedback documented | **Flag for resubmission next cycle** |

**Post-Award Compliance** (stages 7+):
- Auto-generate reporting calendar from grant terms
- Track: quarterly financials, annual narratives, closeout
- Alert: "Your NEA Q2 report is due in 14 days. You spent $12,400 of $50,000."
- Nobody does this. Multi-year retention.

**VALUE ADDED:** Complete lifecycle tracking from discovery to compliance. Post-award tracking is the #1 unmet need in the market.
